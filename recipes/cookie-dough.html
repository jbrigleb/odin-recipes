Edible Cookie Dough

Description

A paraphrased recipe for edible cookie dough.

You can enjoy cookie dough without making the actual cookies.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • butter
  • vanilla
  • salt
  • milk
  • chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat together sugar and butter
  2. Beat in vanilla and salt
  3. Add flour
  4. Mix until crumbly
  5. Add milk and stir
  6. Stir in chocolate chips
